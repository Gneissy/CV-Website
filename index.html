Lost Ark Craft

This app is used for maximizing the profit of crafting in the game Lost Ark.

The whole point is to track both Trade and Battle items' prices in order to calculate the most profitable battle item that can be crafted.

The idea here is to create a public calculation form that doesn't require everyone to do it all over again.

App is hosted via render, so it may last around 10-20s for page to load.
